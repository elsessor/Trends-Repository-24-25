Finding Dormy

Company

Welcome to Finding Dormy! Our platform offers a user-friendly interface where users can browse available dorms, access essential details such as amenities, safety features, and proximity to schools and universities. Whether you're new to the city or just looking for a better living option, Finding Dormy makes your search stress-free and efficient. We understand the challenges students face when searching for a safe, comfortable, and affordable place to stay. That's why we developed an innovative app designed to help students find dormitories around their area with ease.

Many students, particularly those new to a city, face the challenge of finding suitable and available dorms or boarding houses near their desired location. This can be a time-consuming and frustrating process, involving personal inquiries or extensive online searches. As a result, students may end up spending too much time and money on temporary accommodations, or settle for suboptimal options that don't meet their needs. By providing a comprehensive and user-friendly platform for finding nearby dorms or boarding houses, our app aims to alleviate this problem and make the process of finding suitable accommodations easier and more efficient.

Meet The Team

We are a team of students and tech enthusiasts committed to simplifying dorm hunting through a reliable and user-friendly platform. Driven by firsthand experience, we aim to connect students with safe and affordable living spaces effortlessly.
